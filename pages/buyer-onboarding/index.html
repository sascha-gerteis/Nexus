Buyer onboarding sequence.

A simple five-email path Nexus can send after buyer signup so new users understand how to choose and launch their first automation.

Browse marketplaceRequest workflow review
Email 1

Welcome to Nexus

Explain the marketplace: products show outputs, setup needs, support path, messages, and dashboard history.

Email 2

Pick your first automation

Ask what manual work repeats most often and link to marketplace categories plus the recommendation quiz.

Email 3

Why output preview matters

Teach buyers to compare expected output, required inputs, and setup path before checkout.

Email 4

How guided setup works

Explain self-serve vs guided setup, credential reminders, and what to prepare before setup.

Email 5

Get a workflow review

Invite the buyer to describe the manual process if no ready-made product fits.

Later

Dashboard reminder

Show where orders, outputs, messages, reviews, and cancellation/support paths live.
