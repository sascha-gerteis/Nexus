Before you contact Nexus.

These answers help buyers, builders, and partners understand how Nexus handles product questions, guided setup, custom requests, and developer inquiries.

Can I buy a product directly right now?

Yes. Nexus supports marketplace products, checkout, setup forms, buyer dashboards, and messaging. Some products may still need guided setup depending on the process and tools involved.

What is Nexus guided setup?

Guided setup means Nexus helps prepare, configure, test, and launch the product instead of leaving the customer alone with technical steps. This is best when the business wants the result without managing the setup details.

Can Nexus handle a custom process?

Yes. If the marketplace does not have the right product, send a custom request. Include the current manual process, the tools involved, and the result you want.

Can builders submit products?

Yes. Builders can join the developer waitlist for review. Approved developers can create profiles, submit products, and have products reviewed before they go live.

What should I include in my message?

Tell us what you want automated, what tools or data are involved, how often it should run, and what output you expect. For example: a PDF report, dashboard update, lead list, alert, handoff note, or weekly summary.
