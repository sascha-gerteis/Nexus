Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions about our solutions.

What platforms do you support for reports?

We support Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, TikTok, and YouTube. You can provide access through analytics platforms, exports, or screenshots.

Where can I deploy the chatbot?

Our chatbots can be deployed on websites, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, Slack, and through API integration with custom applications.

What's the turnaround time for reports?

Starter: 48 hours. Growth: 24 hours. Executive: 12 hours (priority). Add-ons may add 2-4 hours depending on complexity.

How long does chatbot setup take?

Basic setup: 2-3 days. Pro with custom training: 5-7 days.

Can we white-label the solutions?

Yes, both reports and chatbots can be white-labeled. Reports have a +$39 add-on, chatbots include white-labeling in the Pro package.

Can we do monthly retainers?

Yes! Both reports and chatbots offer monthly subscription models with discounted rates for long-term commitments.
