Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions about our reports.

What platforms do you support?

We support Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, TikTok, and YouTube. You can provide access through analytics platforms, exports, or screenshots.

What do you need from us to start?

We need access to your analytics (read-only) or exported data. For most platforms, we can work with screenshots if needed. We'll provide clear instructions after purchase.

What's the turnaround time?

Starter: 48 hours. Growth: 24 hours. Executive: 12 hours (priority). Add-ons may add 2-4 hours depending on complexity.

Can we white-label the reports?

Yes, the White-label + Cover Page add-on (+$39) removes all Nexus.ai branding and adds your logo, colors, and company information.

Can we do this monthly?

Yes, we offer monthly retainers at a discounted rate. Contact us after your first report to set up a recurring schedule.

Can you include ads/ROI analysis?

The Executive package includes ROI analysis. For ads-specific insights, add the "Paid Ads Check" add-on (+$49) to any package.

What if our data is incomplete?

We'll work with what you have and note any data limitations in the report. Our analysis focuses on actionable insights from available data.

Is this a template or customized?

Each report is customized to your data. We use a professional framework but all insights, visuals, and recommendations are specific to your performance.
