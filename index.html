Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions about our solutions.

Can I purchase products individually?

Yes! All three products are available for individual purchase. Social Listening ($299/month), Social Media Reports ($199/month), and Chatbot ($399/month). No contracts, month-to-month.

Are there bundle discounts?

Yes! Save when you bundle: Social Listening + Social Media Reports = $399/month (save $99). All three products = $699/month (save $198).

What's the turnaround time for reports?

Social Media Reports: 48-hour delivery. Social Listening: Set up within 24 hours. Chatbot: 2-3 days for basic, 5-7 days for Pro with custom training.

How long does chatbot setup take?

Basic setup: 2-3 days. Pro with custom training: 5-7 days.

Can we white-label the solutions?

Yes, all products can be white-labeled. Reports have a +$39 add-on, chatbots include white-labeling in the Pro package, and listening white-labeling is available as an add-on.

Can we do monthly retainers?

Yes! All products offer monthly subscription models. Contact us for discounted rates for annual commitments or multi-product bundles.
