Common questions

Before you contact Nexus.

These answers help businesses, developers, and partners understand the current Phase 1 marketplace and what will happen next.

Can I buy an automation directly right now?

Yes. Nexus now supports checkout, setup forms, and automation outputs through the buyer dashboard. Some products may still require Nexus guided install depending on the setup requirements.

What is Nexus Guided Install?

Nexus Guided Install means Nexus helps configure and prepare the automation instead of leaving the customer alone with a workflow, API keys, setup instructions, and possible errors. This is best for companies that want the result without touching the technical setup.

Can Nexus build a custom automation?

Yes. If the marketplace does not yet have what you need, send a custom automation request. Include the manual process, the tools involved, and the output you want.

Can developers submit automations?

Developer submissions are not fully open yet. Developers can join the waitlist now. Later, approved developers will be able to submit automations with previews, setup requirements, customization options, and product information.

What should I include in my message?

Tell us what you want automated, what tools or data are involved, how often it should run, and what output you expect. For example: a PDF report, dashboard, chatbot response, lead list, alert, or weekly summary.
