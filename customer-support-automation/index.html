Buyer FAQ

Does automation send replies automatically?

Some products draft replies only; others can send if the buyer explicitly approves that setup path.

Can it detect urgent issues?

Yes, based on the product rules, keywords, sentiment, priority, customer tier, or custom criteria.

What counts as a bug?

If the delivered support output no longer matches the shown output structure, Nexus reviews the order.

What if I need a new routing rule?

Small setup fixes may be support. New business logic can be a custom request.
