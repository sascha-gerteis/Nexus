Buyer FAQ

Will this replace my CRM?

No. Most lead automations connect to the CRM or spreadsheet you already use.

What do I need before setup?

Your lead source, destination tool, routing rules, and the follow-up summary you want.

Can Nexus handle several lead sources?

Yes, but multi-source routing may need guided setup or a custom workflow review.

What happens if a lead source changes?

Nexus reviews the order and explains whether it is a setup fix, bug, or new custom request.
